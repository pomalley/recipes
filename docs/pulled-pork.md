Source: [a sweet pea chef](https://www.asweetpeachef.com/instant-pot-pulled-pork/) - I made it lazier.

## Ingredients

- 4 lb pork loin
- 2 tbsp cooking oil

**Dry Ingredients**

- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1 to 2 tsp cumin

**Sauce / Wet Ingredients**

- 1/2 cup broth or water
- Tomatoes: 4 oz can of sauce; or 1/2 to 1 cup canned crushed/diced/etc
- garlic cloves, minced or crushed
- 1 tbsp molasses
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1/2 tbsp (apple cider) vinegar
- salt and pepper

## Directions

- Cut pork into four pieces.
- Put dry ingredients together and coat pork chunks with the mix.
- Put oil in the instant pot and set to saute.
  - When hot, sear pork chunks for ~2 minutes each side.
- Remove pork and deglaze the instant pot with water/broth.
- Add remaining sauce ingredients, and cook until heated through.
- Return pork chunks to instant pot.
- Pressure cook for 60 minutes, and let release for 20 minutes.
- Shred pork (in a large bowl) with forks; add sauce to taste.
